Relational Evangelism and Discipleship (RED) Approach

Long term strategy to share our faith with our clients when offering medical services and linking them to bible teaching churches, in line with John 10:10b.
We also invite them to our weekly bible teaching services, on Wednesday morning.

Tenets of RED approach

Relational

Creating positive and lasting bonds in our interactions with one another

Evangelism

Magnifying Christ through loving, caring and kind deeds

Discipleship

Promoting personal and corporate growth in the knowledge of and service to our Lord and Saviour
